Thermy Therm’s Café makes food that fills the SPIRIT! ...

Thermy Therm’s Café is a food truck/trailer mobile service. Our company was created to bring healthy, tasty, and fun foods to the world! Therman’s passion for food is started as a young boy in Oakland, CA. He loved cooking with my friends as well as watching my Mother and Grandmother cook. Therman learned at a young age that food brings people together and spreads love.

Therman has over 10 plus years of experience in the restaurant industry, working in areas of a line cook, production chef, food management and certified bartending in Phoenix, AZ. He has always had a passion for cooking. He recently retired from Southwest Airlines after 28 years of service. This was the opportunity to pursue his dream and Thermy Therm’s Café was born.
